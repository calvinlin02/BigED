Donating doesn't need to be difficult


BigED aims to enable avid and prospective donors to discover different campaigns to support different aspects of education, such as school supplies, equipment, meals and girls' education.

BigED offer convenience and choices for donors to book pick-ups and drop-off times for physical donations and offering those who want to purchase one-time or subscription model donations instead.
