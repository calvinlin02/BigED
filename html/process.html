Domain: Donations


Project Type: Interface Design for IOS Mobile Devices

Project Brief:


Conduct research, propose ideas, design prototype, assess design, and refine an interactive system tailored for a particular domain and context.


Why Donations?

Few donation apps have shown to have good UI design and even fewer had good UX

Highlight an area that is poorly explored, but noble

Motivated by personal goals and values

Who Would Use BigED?


Avid donors who wish to set up recurring payments


What we learned

What users want


  • tranparency
  • knowing their donations are used well
  • knowing their impact
  • convenience

What users hate


  • Felt donations are not contributing much
  • Overwhelmed with options
  • Disorganized apps

Stage 2: Style Guide


Stage 3: User Testing


Through user testing, we have identified the most pressing issues as follows: a confusing credit system, privacy concerns during the onboarding process, poor hierarchy in some sections, as well as some unwanted features such as information-dense graphs.
